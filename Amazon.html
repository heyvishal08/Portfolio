Amazon Engagement Optimization Dashboard

"Where others see numbers, I see possibilities."

Project Information

  • Name: Amazon Engagement Optimization Dashboard
  • Project Date: March, 2025

Summary

This project analyzes Amazon's user engagement trends, cart abandonment rates, and conversion performance using Power BI and data analytics. The insights help in optimizing user retention, improving conversions, and understanding customer behavior across different traffic sources.

By leveraging data-driven strategies, businesses can enhance user experience, increase sales, and refine marketing efforts for better engagement.

Objective

  • ✔️Track user engagement trends over time to understand how users interact with the platform and identify patterns that drive engagement.
  • ✔️Analyze factors influencing cart abandonment and conversion rates to uncover key bottlenecks in the purchasing journey and improve checkout completion.
  • ✔️Evaluate the performance of different devices and traffic sources to determine which channels drive the highest engagement and conversions, enabling better marketing strategy optimization.
  • ✔️Provide actionable, data-driven recommendations that help Amazon enhance user retention, reduce drop-offs, and maximize overall sales performance.

Tools Used


  • ✔️Power BI – Designed and developed interactive dashboards with rich visualizations for better insights and decision-making.
  • ✔️ DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) – Created custom calculations and measures to analyze key engagement and sales metrics effectively.
  • ✔️ Excel & SQL – Performed data extraction, cleaning, and transformation to ensure accuracy and consistency in reporting.

Recommendations & Business Impact


  • ✅ Optimize Mobile Experience → Mobile users have high engagement but low conversion. Improve UI/UX & checkout experience.
  • ✅ Reduce Cart Abandonment → Offer discounts, free shipping, and reminders via email retargeting.
  • ✅ Improve Social Media Conversions → Target high-abandonment users with retargeting ads & personalized offers.
  • ✅ Enhance Desktop Performance → Desktop drives the most conversions. Optimize web-based offers & promotions.
  • ✅ Analyze Peak Engagement Hours → Identify when users are most active and schedule marketing campaigns accordingly.
