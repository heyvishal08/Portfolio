Coffee Shop Sales Insights

"The world is one big dataset waiting to be analyzed."

Project Information

  • Name: Coffee Shop Sales Insight
  • Project Date: November, 2024

Summary

This project focuses on analyzing sales data from a coffee shop chain using Power BI and Python (Pandas). The goal is to identify key sales trends, top-selling products, peak sales hours, and store performance.

This analysis helps in making data-driven business decisions to optimize sales strategies and improve overall revenue.
