"In a world full of noise, data is the truth that speaks the loudest."

Project Information

  • Name: Blinkit Sales Insight
  • Project Date: January, 2025

Summary

This project is a comprehensive analysis of Blinkit's sales trends, customer purchasing behavior, and outlet performance. By leveraging Power BI and data analytics, the project provides deep insights into various aspects of the business, helping stakeholders make data-driven decisions.

Through detailed data visualization and exploration, the analysis helps identify high-performing products, seasonal sales trends, and customer buying habits.

Objective

This project aims to conduct an in-depth analysis of Blinkit's online grocery business by examining sales trends, customer purchasing behavior, and outlet performance. The primary focus is to uncover valuable insights that can help optimize business operations and drive growth.

Recommendations & Business Impact

To maximize growth and optimize Blinkit's sales strategy, the following data-driven recommendations have been identified:

  • üí∞ Expand Supermarket Type 1 Stores ‚Äì These outlets generate the highest revenue, making them a key focus for expansion. More stores of this type can boost overall profitability.
  • üìç Strengthen Presence in Tier 3 Cities ‚Äì 39% of total sales come from small cities, proving high demand in these areas. Investing in logistics and local marketing can further increase revenue potential.
  • ü•ó Stock More Low-Fat Products ‚Äì With 65% of customers preferring low-fat options, ensuring a steady supply of these products can drive higher sales and customer retention.
  • üõí Boost Sales for Dairy & Frozen Foods ‚Äì These categories are underperforming despite their importance. Targeted promotions, discounts, and improved inventory placement can enhance their visibility and demand.
  • üì¢ Improve Marketing for New Outlets (2020 & 2022) ‚Äì Newly established outlets show lower sales compared to older ones. Stronger promotional campaigns, introductory offers, and customer engagement strategies can help increase footfall and revenue.
